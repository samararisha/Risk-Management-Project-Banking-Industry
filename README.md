## 📖 Project Overview
This repository contains a comprehensive **Information Security Risk Management (ISRM)** portfolio for a simulated Egyptian commercial bank. The project demonstrates the application of international frameworks and local Central Bank of Egypt (CBE) regulations to identify, assess, and mitigate financial sector risks.

## 📁 Repository Structure
* `01_Methodology/`: Risk assessment criteria and scoring matrices.
* `02_Asset_Inventory/`: Categorization of banking "Crown Jewels."
* `03_Risk_Register/`: Detailed analysis of threats, vulnerabilities, and residual risks.
* `04_Treatment_Plan/`: Mitigation strategies and control mapping (NIST/ISO).
* `05_Reports/`: Executive summaries and Heat Maps for stakeholders.

## 🛠️ Frameworks & Regulations Applied
* **ISO/IEC 27005:** Risk Management methodology.
* **CBE Cybersecurity Framework:** National banking compliance.
* **PCI DSS v4.0:** Payment card industry security standards.
* **Egypt Law No. 151/2020:** Personal Data Protection Law.
